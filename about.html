Education

I graduated in 1985 from the Beijing University of Chinese Medicine, the most reputable Chinese medicine school in China. I have practiced Chinese Medicine at Beijing Hospital, one of the prestigious and highly regarded hospitals in China.

I was senior attending physician of integrative (Chinese and Western) Medicine. During this time I got a high quality of training in internal medicine, especially digestive diseases, pulmonary diseases, endocrine and metabolic disorders.

As a NCCAOM certified acupuncturist and Chinese Herbologist, I am more than capable of helping you regain or maintain your health.

Previous Experience

In addition to my work above, I have also worked at OCOM (Oregon College of Oriental Medicine) as a clinical supervisor.

I have also done extensive medical research for the University of Virginia and OHSU.
